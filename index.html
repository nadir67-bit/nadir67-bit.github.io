What we Serve

Our Best Services

At Curelink Medical Billing, we understand that the medical billing process can be complex and time consuming. That’s why we offer a wide variety of services that are designed to streamline and simplify the process. From claims processing to coding and reimbursement, we provide our clients with everything they need to ensure their medical billing operations run smoothly and efficiently.

Comprehensive Medical Billing Services

Our experienced billing team will ensure that your claims are processed and paid accurately, on time, every time. We provide comprehensive medical billing services, including: • Claim filing • Coding and billing • Processing insurance payments • Follow-up on unpaid and rejected claims • Manage patient accounts • Submit appeals.

Electronic Medical Records (EMR)

Our EMR system makes it easier to manage patient records, track appointment schedules and access patient information. Features include: • Secure and confidential patient data • Quick and easy access to patient medical records.

Payment Processing Solutions

Our payment processing solutions are designed to make it easy for your patients to pay their medical bills online. Features include: • Multiple payment options, including credit cards, debit cards and ACH payments • Real-time authorization and capture of payments.
